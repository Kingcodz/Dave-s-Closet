Care Instructions

Hand wash or dry clean only. Iron while damp for best results. Natural wrinkles are part of linen's charm - embrace them!

Dry clean recommended. If hand washing, use mild detergent and cold water. Never wring - roll in towel to remove excess water and lay flat to dry.

Machine wash cold with similar colors. Tumble dry low or line dry. Iron on medium heat if needed.
